### Rob Hubbard ##############################################################

/Hubbard_Rob/
COMMENT: All of these tunes have been confirmed by Rob Hubbard to be his. 
         Hubbard's own comments are denoted by (RH).

/Classics/Hubbard_Rob/Extirpator.sap
COMMENT: "Extirpator is unknown to me, but the tune is Sanxion, which is 
         probably my favorite tune I ever wrote on the c64." (RH)

/Classics/Hubbard_Rob/Jet_Set_Willy.sap
COMMENT: "Jet Set Willy 2 was unique to the Atari and is very much in the Jarre 
         style." (RH)

/Classics/Hubbard_Rob/International_Karate.sap
COMMENT: "I.K. was the first tune I ever did on the Atari, which was also quite 
         a challenge because of the 8bit pitch registers." (RH)

/Classics/Hubbard_Rob/Warhawk.sap
COMMENT: "Warhawk was a c64 tune originally called Proteus." (RH)
