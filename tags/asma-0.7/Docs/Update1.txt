ASMA Update #1 (pre-release)

These rips together with the original ASMA collection will make version 0.7
of ASMA (out of version 0.6).

Number of tunes: 132

Most new tunes come from the "SAP09.zip" archive from an unknown ripper (I
must ask SoTe who ripped them :-). Also some tunes from the "universal"
directories were moved to the ones they really belong to. Last but not least,
some credits were completed in many tunes.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------
Note: This is the pre-release update of ASMA, thus it will be not counted as
      update when the first official ASMA archive will be released.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
